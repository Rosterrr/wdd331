Why visit El Salvador?

Natural variety

Travelers can enjoy volcanoes, lakes, beaches, and mountain towns in short distances.

Culture and food

Pupusas, local markets, and community traditions give every region its own character.

Travel tips

  • Bring lightweight clothes and comfortable shoes.
  • Plan beach and volcano visits early in the day.
  • Carry cash for some small shops and local businesses.
  • Respect local communities and protected natural areas.
