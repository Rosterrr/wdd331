🌋 Explore El Salvador

Site Name

Explore El Salvador

This name was chosen because it clearly represents a tourism website focused on discovering the beauty, culture, beaches, volcanoes, and attractions of El Salvador. It is short, memorable, and directly connected to the purpose of helping visitors explore the country.

Optional domain availability: exploreelsalvador.org

Site Purpose

The purpose of this website is to provide visitors with useful travel information about El Salvador. The site will highlight popular tourist destinations, cultural attractions, natural landmarks, and travel tips. It will help users learn where to go, what to do, and why El Salvador is a great place to visit.

Scenarios

  • What are the best places to visit in El Salvador for beaches and volcano views?
  • Where can I find information about local food, culture, and tourist activities in El Salvador?

Color Schema

The website will use the following colors:

#0B3C5D
#F4A261
#2A9D8F
  • #0B3C5D – used for headings, header, footer, and navigation background.
  • #F4A261 – used for buttons, highlights, logo accent, and important sections.
  • #2A9D8F – used for borders, accents, and secondary visual emphasis.

Typography

  • Montserrat – used for headings, titles, and logo text.
  • Open Sans – used for body text, paragraphs, and general page content.
